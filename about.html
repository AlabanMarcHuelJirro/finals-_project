THE BAYAWAK STORY

The Bayawak (Monitor Lizard)- resilient, adaptable, and built for survival.

Bayawak Clothing was born from the grind, inspired by the bayawak, a creature that survives and thrives in the harshest conditions. It moves with purpose, adapts without apology, and carries its strength in silence. That’s the energy we bring to every thread.

Our designs are rooted in Filipino street culture: raw, expressive, and unfiltered. We create for those who move boldly, who wear their story with pride, and who find grace in the everyday hustle. Bayawak is not just a brand, it’s a movement, a mindset, a tribute to the streets that raised us.

Every piece we release is a reminder: you were built for this. You were made to move.

“I started Bayawak to create something that spoke to our roots, our hustle, and our identity. Every piece is a tribute to the street and the grace of Filipino street culture. I wanted to build something that felt raw and real, something that didn’t just follow trends, but told stories. Stories of movement, of struggle, of pride. Bayawak is for those who grind quietly, who move boldly, and who wear their culture with honor.”

Marc Huel Jirro Alaban – Founder
